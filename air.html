Air pollution refers to release of Pollutants into the air that are detrimental to human health and the planet as a whole

Causes of air pollution

  • Burning Fossil Fuel
  • Agriculture activities
  • Exhaust from factories and industry
  • Mining operation
  • Indoor air pollution

Prevention measures

  • Reduce air pollution of are automobile
  • Use eco-friendly products in our home
  • Plant trees
  • Keep air-cleaning houseplants
  • Take a good care of your fireplace
  • Recycled and by recycle products
