Water pollution can be difined as the contamination of the water bodies such as lakes, rivers, oceans and groundwater.

Causes of Water Pollution

  • Sewage and waste water
  • Industrial waste.
  • Oil pollution
  • Acid Rain
  • Global warming

Prevention measures

  • Sewage treatments
  • Prevent river water from getting polluted
  • Strict adherence to water laws
  • Routine cleaning
  • Reducing the use of herbicides pesticide
