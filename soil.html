Soil pollution is the degradation of the natural environment of soil and various pollutants. These pollutants include pesticide, insecticide, industrial and household waste lead from bacteria's nuclear waste, and sewage slush.

Causes of soil pollution

  • Deforestation
  • Dumping of waste
  • Industrial Activities
  • Leaking sewers:-Underground tanks,and underground pipelines.

Prevention measures

  • Forest plantation.
  • Weed control
  • Reuse and recycle
  • Proper solid waste treatments
  • Promote uses of Bio Pesticide and Fungicides
